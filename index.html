Creating a healthy lifestyle doesn't have to mean drastic changes.

What Is a Healthy Lifestyle?
You know the obvious behaviors that describe someone who is healthy and takes care of themselves. A healthy person doesn't smoke, tries to maintain a healthy weight, eats healthy foods with plenty of fruits, vegetables and fiber and, of course, exercises on a regular basis.
Does a healthy lifestyle make a difference?
As it turns out, healthy habits make a big difference. A mega-analysis of 15 international studies that included over 500,000 participants found that over half of premature deaths were due to unhealthy lifestyle factors such as poor diet, inactivity, obesity, excessive alcohol intake, and smoking.
What Are 10 Tips for a Healthy Lifestyle?
Here are 10 tips for a healthy lifestyle: eat a balanced diet, stay well hydrated, exercise regularly, get enough good sleep, limit your alcohol intake, do not smoke, protect yourself from the sun, wash your hands, manage your stress, practice safe sex.
